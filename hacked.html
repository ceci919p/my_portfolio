Hacked Hogwarts

Have you ever dreamed of being the administrator of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry? I have built a system to help you become exactly that – and if you feel adventurous you might find some secrets along the way.

The Process

In this project we had to build a system to help administrators handle student lists. The requirements for the task were to provide an interface to show the student list with filtering and sorting options. When clicking on a student a popup window with detailed information about each student should be shown. There should also be an option to expel students and see a list of expelled students. The administrator should be able to make two students for each house a prefect and this should show in their popup. In addition, some students can join the inquisitorial squad based on blood-status. After these requirements were met, we had to “hack” the system, inject ourselves to the list and make us unable to be expelled. We also had to break the inquisitorial squad by removing students shortly after they had been added. Finally, we had to mess up the pure-blood algorithm.

Code Planning

Since this project had a lot of requirements, I decided to split it up in 4 parts. To plan the code, I made activity diagrams for each of the parts.

Part 1

I started coding the display of the student with filtering, sorting, searching and popup window.

Part 2

The next part was planning and coding how to expel students and how to make them prefects. When making the prefect function we had to make some rules. Only two students – one girl and one boy - from each house could be prefects.

Part 3

The third part was planning and then coding how to get the blood-status and display them in the popup. In addition, we had to make the inquisitorial squad function. Only purebloods could be part of this squad, so we had to check the student’s blood-status and make a modal warning if the student didn’t meet the requirements.

Part 4

The final part was the hacking where I made a function called hackTheSystem(). The first thing to happen was injecting me to the student list. To make sure I couldn’t be expelled I made a rule saying that the hacker couldn’t be expelled. In the second part I had to randomize the blood-status of pure-bloods and making the half- and muggle-bloods listed as pure-bloods. Finally, I had to make the inquisitorial squad only work for a short amount of time so that the removal was noticeable.

Design

There weren’t many requirements for the visual design other than making a theme in the popup for each house. I went with the red, blue, green, and yellow colors from the franchise. When designing the Hogwarts crest, I used the same colors as the popup themes. I based the design of the crest from the movies and simplified it removing smaller details and focusing on colors, shapes, and shadows of animals. The different house crests are drawn in the same style but has a few extra minor details. I also designed the search icon which consists of a magnifying glass with a feather instead of a handle. The feather was to make it look more magical and as if it is hovering in the air. To ideate a design for the prefect and inquisitorial squad badges I watched the movies and searched for merchandise. The result of the research is what the designs are based on – I just made my own interpretation of them. I used classical, extravagant, and dramatic shapes and details to make it look almost antique and match the visual identity of Hogwarts.
